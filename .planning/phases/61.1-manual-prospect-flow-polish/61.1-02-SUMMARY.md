## Decisions Made

- Google s2/favicons is primary (covers ~99% of domains), DuckDuckGo ip3 is secondary fallback — both accept HEAD requests
- `buildInlineGoogleFaviconUrl` is exported as a pure builder without probing — the browser's own `onError` on `<img>` serves as the "probe" in ProspectLogo, avoiding a double-probe on render
- `AbortSignal.timeout(3000)` used directly (Node 18+ / modern browsers) — simpler than AbortController + setTimeout pattern, matches codebase convention
