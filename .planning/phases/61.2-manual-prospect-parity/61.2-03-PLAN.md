<objective>
Give Romano a way to pre-populate known company metadata when creating prospects manually — avoiding the hollow detail card problem where manual prospects show 12+ blank Apollo fields. The form stays one-click for the common case (section is collapsed by default); expanding reveals 6 optional enrichment fields.

Purpose: Marfa smoke test found `companyName`, `industry`, `description`, `city`, `country`, `employeeRange` all blank for manually-created prospects. Romano knows these facts for Marfa. Entering them at create-time populates the detail card immediately — no enrichment needed.

Output:

- `admin.createAndProcess` Zod input schema extended with 6 optional nullable enrichment fields
- Sticky guard: if `input.companyName` is provided, Apollo enrichment results must NOT overwrite it
- `/admin/prospects/new` form gets a collapsible `<details>` section ("Optionele verrijking") with 6 fields
- Form uses plain `useState` (the established pattern — no react-hook-form, no Zod on the client)
- `country` field defaults to "Nederland"
  </objective>
