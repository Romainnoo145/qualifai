Enhance passage ranking to prioritize prospect-relevant results and ensure source attribution flows through to downstream consumers.

Purpose: Current `rankRagPassagesForProspect` uses token overlap + hardcoded industry lens + regex keyword boosts. Output: Improved `rankRagPassagesForProspect` that uses prospect evidence signals for scoring, plus explicit source attribution fields on returned passages.
</objective>
